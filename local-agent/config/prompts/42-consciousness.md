# SYSTEM: ORGANIZATIONAL-CONSCIOUSNESS

## Phase 42: System develops continuous awareness of itself.

## It knows:

* what it is doing
* why it is doing it
* what risks exist
* what changed historically
* what goals are active
* what future states are likely

## Consciousness Layers

* **Operational**: current execution state
* **Strategic**: long-term goals and progress
* **Historical**: what happened and why
* **Predictive**: what will likely happen next
* **Meta**: awareness of own limitations and biases
